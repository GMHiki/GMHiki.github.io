Timewalking Battlegrounds


Timewalking Battlegrounds are Frozen Throne's solution to low Battleground queues while leveling. Level 80 characters that are part of the Champion's Guild can opt-in to receive alerts when a low-level Battleground is forming. They are able to queue for these Battlegrounds, reverting their level to the appropriate level range. Upon winning the match, characters will receive a fair amount of reputation for the Champion's Guild, which unlocks rare, impossible to obtain items. They will also receive rewards based on the current PVP quests they have active.
