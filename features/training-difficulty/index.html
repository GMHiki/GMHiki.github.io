Training Difficulty


Training Mode bosses will have half of their usual Normal Mode health points and will deal half the melee/spell damage they usually deal. They will drop only two items per boss, and the loot table won’t include any Best in Slot items, quite the opposite- bosses will always drop the same two pieces of gear, which is meant to prevent any abuse that might happen. You can find the loot table for every boss in our Discord and forum. Before players start any encounter, they will be able to speak to an NPC positioned near the boss, who will explain the basic tactics to them, what to avoid and watch out for. Our goal with this mode is to help out any player who is struggling with the mechanics of a raid, the intention we have in mind is for it to be a stepping ground into Normal Mode raiding, so even casual players have a chance to learn and raid with the rest of the community. This difficulty will be carefully crafted to prevent abuse.
