Champion's Guild


Frozen Throne has re-invented WotLK PVP Progression.

After hitting level 10, your character will receive mail, inviting you to the Champion's Guild. Champion's Guild NPCs are located in every major city. Prove your mettle against the Champion's Guilds challenges and unlock a wide array of unique PVP features.

True PVP Leveling, BG & Arena Satchels, Timewalking Battlegrounds, Solo Queue Arena, Unobtainable PVP Transmog, Rare Titles, and more.

Once your character is level 80, you will unlock a new reputation system for the Champion's Guild, and progression increases rarity of these items! Tournaments, PVP Events, and Custom PVP Quests will become unlocked for Champion's Guild members.

But above all, the Champion's Guild values honor and respect. If members are found breaking the Champion's Guild honor code & server rules, they will be entered into a strike system, which may ultimately end in a dishonorable discharge from the Champion's Guild. Any member kicked from the Champion's Guild will no longer be able to participate in any of Frozen Throne's PVP Features and Events. So fight honorably, and treat your enemies with respect -- they may be your allies in other matches!
